Welcome to Little Lemon

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant located in the heart of Chicago. Our eclectic menu is inspired by Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisine with a modern twist. Come join us for a taste of the Mediterranean!

About Little Lemon

Our Team

Our team members bring years of experience serving authentic Mediterranean cuisine. We have assembled a team that is passionate about food and eager to share their expertise with you.

Heritage & Vision

Little Lemon is a family-owned restaurant founded by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian. They dreamed of sharing their love of Mediterranean food with Chicagoans, and brought authentic family recipes to the table with a modern twist.

Quality Ingredients

We use fresh ingredients sourced from local markets whenever possible. Our chefs carefully select the best produce, meats, and cheeses to bring you the most delicious Mediterranean dishes.

Our Chefs' Inspiration

Italian Countryside

Mario draws inspiration from his childhood in rural Italy, where his family grew fresh vegetables and made homemade pasta.

Greek Islands

Adrian fell in love with the Greek Islands while studying abroad. He brings the flavors of the Aegean Sea to Little Lemon.

Turkish Bazaar

The hustle and bustle of Istanbul's markets inspire many of our dishes. Come taste the exotic flavors of the Mediterranean!

Meet the Owners

Mario

Mario is a trained chef from Italy. He brings his family's authentic Mediterranean recipes to Little Lemon.

Adrian

Adrian is in charge of marketing and expansion efforts. He is passionate about Mediterranean cuisine and bringing Little Lemon to new heights.
